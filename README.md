## üí° Study Tips

### During Video Study (Jan 17 - Feb 13)
1. **Watch videos in full** - the system tracks your progress
2. **Take quizzes immediately** after each video (marked with ‚úÖ)
3. **Aim for 100%** on all quizzes before moving on
4. **Re-watch if needed** - understanding > rushing
5. **Take notes** on key concepts
6. **Stay consistent** - daily 75-90 minute sessions

### During Practice Tests (Feb 20 - Apr 1)
1. **Take practice exams regularly** - every 2-3 days minimum
2. **Review immediately** after each exam while fresh
3. **Focus on WHY answers are correct**, not just memorizing
4. **Re-watch videos** for specific topics you miss
5. **Retake quizzes** for any weak areas identified
6. **Simulate test conditions** for timed exams
7. **Track your progress** - aim for consistent improvement to 90%+

## üéì Practice Test Strategy

After completing all videos (Feb 13), you'll have 41 days for intensive practice:

### Week 1 (Feb 20-28): Initial Assessment
- Take 3 practice exams to identify weak areas
- Review results immediately after each exam
- Re-watch videos and retake quizzes for weak topics

### Weeks 2-3 (Mar 1-14): Intensive Practice
- Take timed practice exam every 2-3 days
- Review incorrect answers immediately (understand WHY)
- Study Transport Canada sample questions
- Re-watch specific videos for missed topics

### Weeks 4-5 (Mar 15-28): Exam Simulation
- Take full timed exams under test conditions
- Aim for consistent 90%+ scores
- Practice time management
- Retake difficult quizzes from the course

### Week 6 (Mar 29 - Apr 1): Final Preparation
- Two final practice exams
- Light review of least confident areas
- Early night before test
- Stay relaxed and confident
